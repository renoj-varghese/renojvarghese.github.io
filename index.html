About Me

Hi. My name is Renoj. I am a user experience deigner. My background is in user research, graphic design, animation, and prototyping. I'm best in a interdiscplinary role where I can combine my skills to make something really meaningful.

My Process

It all starts with a defined, specific problem. Next step is to research and understand how to tackle the problem. I draft ideas on paper or on a digital tool. Then, I communicate my ideas to others using wireframes, style guides, and prototypes. Finally, it is time to craft the final deliverables and get feedback Then rinse and repeat as needed :)
