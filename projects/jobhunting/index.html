JobHunting

introduction

The job search process is far from perfect. JobHunting highlights some of the problems jobseekers face.

Goal 1

Talk about what problems people face when searching for a job.

Goal 2

Take a more satrical and comical approach on the situation. Give people a good laugh.

Overview

Role: Motion Designer, Animator

Team: Solo, with help from peers and professors

Duration: 4 Weeks

final animation

JobHunting Animation

inspiration

Personal Experience

This animation was a way for me to open up about my painpoints in the job search process. It was a way to talk about it without having to say anything at all.

Talking to friends

When talking to friends about their experiences with the job search process, we had a lot in common. I incorporated these common experiences into the final animation.

Results

The final animation was featured in the Metropolitan Computer Animation Festival. It also won the "Most Comedic" award in my Motion Graphics class.

Challenges and Lessons

Finding the Right Song

One the main challenges was finding the right audio. A lot of music is copyrighted and could not be used in my animation. It took some time to decide on the right song, but I found the one that had a good sense of humor.

Timing is Everything

Like a dancer has to time themself to the music, I had to time my animation to the audio I chose. Storyboarding and creating After Effects Markers helped me time my animations to the music.
