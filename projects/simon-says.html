Simon Says

A simple game in Unity

My first full Unity Game, in which I designed the UI, made all the art assets in PhotoShop, and coded the logic in Unity. The game is based on the original Simon Says game, where players try to copy a sequence generated by pressing buttons. I had a lot of fun making and playing the game, so I am happy to share it with you.
