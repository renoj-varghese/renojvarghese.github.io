Secret Santa: 3D Printing Someone's Face

Story of one my best projects

My dance team had a Secret Santa event. To make my Secret Santa present, I took of a photo of the recipient from the internet, and drew over there face in Adobe Illustrator as line art. Using the line art and a program known as Cura, I 3D printed the line art portrait. The result is placed in the center with the 4 copies of the line art portrait. This was the display I made for the person who received the gift.
