Projection Mapping on a Mannequin

Background

The idea of the project came from what could be possible with projection mapping technology. I wanted to prototype a new type of store display, in which mannequins could showcase multiple styles. In this example, I chose to showcase sunglass styles.

Process

The mapping process involved tracing over the project to find the shape of the mannequin and its features. Afterwards, I used After Effects to animate a video that showcased different styles of sunglasses. Below is an example of the mask and animation in After Effects.

Final Show

The final show, videoed below, is the result of projecting my animation on to the mannequin. The animation changes the colors of hair, eyebrows, lipstick, and sunglasses to show the potential for accessory store displays.
