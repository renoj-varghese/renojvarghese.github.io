GovContact

A React.js app to find your representative

Getting in contact with legislators is an important way to make sure your voice is heard. GovContact makes it easier to find your representatives by providing the emails, phone numbers, and social media of government officials. By making it easier to find representatives, there can be better communication between legislators and the people they make laws for.

Highlights

User stories

GovContact should make it as easy as possible to find a government representative. As such the user stories were kept short, but in depth to define the app.

Wireframes

After thinking about how someone would use the app, I moved on to wireframes to see what would be the best way to present the information. I chose a card style to show each representative and search bar to find a citizen's address.

Style Tile

The style tile showcases the colors, fonts, and styles for the app. The red, white (and gray), and blue mirror the U.S. flag. The graphics are simple, as to not distract from the main information.

Final Application

The final application was a React.js app, using the Google Civic API as the source of information. Redux helped manage the state of the app.
